POSTER PROJECT

For this assignment the idea was to create a poster of any type of transportation throughout history that has changed. My idea was to create the poster on New York City taxis. The first taxi was of the time period of the approximatly the 1800s. Then the second taxi was for the time peroiod of the 1930s. The next taxi was the period between the 1960s and the 1970s. And then we have the new and improved taxi, the Toyota Camry. Now they have many different types of taxis for NYC mostly all are hybrids. This image was created with both Illustrator and Photoshop.
