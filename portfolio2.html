DIGITAL COLLAGE PROJECT

This was the first project of the class. In this project the idea was to scan ten objects into the computer and create an image from at least five of those scanned objects. For this image I used the opacity tool for many of the scanned objects. I also blurred the edges of many of the objects to create soft edges. This image was created with Photoshop only.
