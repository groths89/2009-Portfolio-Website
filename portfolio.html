AIRPLANE SIGNS PROJECT

For this project the idea was to create a sign dealing with airplanes. This image was made with Illustrator and was made with three other projects. I first traced the objects outline with the pen tool, and I then used the gradient tool to make the nice colors you see on the plane. I also decided to make the backgound of the sign a light blue color. In addition to this sign, there was one made for a person, a blimp, and a dolphin.
